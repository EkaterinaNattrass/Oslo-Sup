About me

Hi there! My name is Katie , and I am a passionate paddle boarder . I recently moved to Oslo in 2021, and I am excited to explore the beautiful waters and fjords of Norway on my board and share my experience with you.

One of the things I love about paddle boarding is how it allows me to connect with nature and take in the beautiful scenery. I love exploring new places on my board, and I am constantly seeking out new locations to paddle.

Through my blog, I hope to share my passion for paddle boarding and SUPs with others. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced paddler, I hope my blog will inspire you to get out on the water and explore the world around you.
